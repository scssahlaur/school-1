Welcome to Saraswati Children School, Sahlaur

-----------------

Saraswati Children School, Sahlaur, is a Primary cum Middle school follow CBSE curriculum. It was founded in 2002. Its a day school with about one thousand students from NUR to Std. VIII. Its offers extracurricular activities (ECA) like cultural fest, debate, painting and many more. The motto of the School is to lead from darkness of ignorance to light of education. Saraswati Children School, Sahlaur promises to provide to provide the finest education to your ward. We are dedicated to make and shape the future of India. Our school is one of best school in state and also in India It is one of the top schools offering world class campus and infrastructure. It is a registered school.
